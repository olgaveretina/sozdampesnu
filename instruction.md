## Pricing Plans

### 1. Just Try It

You receive 4 song versions generated by Suno AI without our refinement.
Price: 600 RUB

Comment:
You will later have the option to upgrade the plan with song refinement according to your instructions.
Delivery time: from 1 hour to 2 days.

---

### 2. I Want a Great Song

Price: 5,000 RUB

We will work separately on your song.
You receive 4 versions with our refinements.

Comment:
You will later have the option to upgrade with publication on Yandex Music and other platforms.
Delivery time: from 2 hours to 2 days.

---

### 3. I Want a Great Song and Publication on Yandex Music and Other Platforms

Price: 15,000 RUB

If this plan is selected, two additional fields appear:

* Cover description for AI generation
  OR
* Image to generate from
  OR
* Ready-made cover (3000×3000 px)

One of these three fields is required.

Comments:

1. When the song and cover are ready, 4 versions will appear in your personal account. You must select the desired version for publication. You will be able to request revisions.
2. Publication time: approximately 10 days after you select the song and cover.
3. If the song generates income, it is fully yours. Withdrawal will be available in your distributor account at Songmoney.
4. Songs created using AI do not always pass moderation. If the song is rejected by platforms, we will refund 50% of the cost. You will still keep your song files. Why not 100%? We spend time and resources on generation, cover creation, and publication attempts.

## Order Statuses

* New (order created, waiting in queue)
* In Progress (our employee started working on your song)
* Song Generated (result available in personal account)
* Sent for Revision (waiting in revision queue)
* Under Revision (employee started revision)
* In Publication Queue (song ready, waiting for publication)
* Publishing in Progress (employee started publication process)
* Sent to Distributor (uploaded to Songmoney, awaiting review)
* Approved by Distributor (first review passed, release date assigned – XXX. If platforms approve, it will be published.)
* Rejected by Distributor (distributor declined the song)
* Rejected by Platforms (AI song did not pass platform moderation)
* Order Completed (work fully completed)

On the order page:

* Full status history with admin comments
* Separate chat dialog where user can message the administrator
